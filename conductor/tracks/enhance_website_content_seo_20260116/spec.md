# Specification: Enhance Website Content and SEO for EH Services Showcase

## 1. Introduction

This track focuses on comprehensively enhancing the existing EH Services website by updating and expanding its content, as well as implementing strategic SEO improvements. The goal is to solidify the website's role as a primary showcase for the company's services and portfolio, aligning with the brand identity of professionalism, reliability, and innovative design, while also improving its discoverability by the target audience.

## 2. Objectives

*   **Improve Content Quality & Depth:** Ensure all service descriptions are detailed, informative, and persuasive, clearly articulating benefits and processes.
*   **Showcase Portfolio Effectively:** Integrate a curated portfolio with in-depth case studies that highlight problem-solution-result narratives and client testimonials.
*   **Optimize for Search Engines:** Implement on-page and technical SEO best practices to improve organic search rankings and increase relevant traffic.
*   **Enhance User Engagement:** Ensure content is engaging and encourages potential clients to explore services and contact EH Services.
*   **Align with Brand Guidelines:** Ensure all new and updated content adheres to the established professional, authoritative, and informative tone, while also embracing the modern and cutting-edge visual aesthetic.
*   **Privacy Compliance:** Confirm all data handling practices within new content or forms remain compliant with GDPR and other relevant privacy regulations.

## 3. Scope

### In-Scope

*   Review and revision of existing service pages to add detailed benefits, processes, and calls to action.
*   Creation of new dedicated pages for any unrepresented core services.
*   Development of a dedicated "Portfolio" section with multiple case studies, each following the problem-solution-result-testimonial format.
*   Integration of client testimonials across relevant sections of the website.
*   Implementation of SEO best practices:
    *   Keyword research and integration into content.
    *   Optimization of page titles, meta descriptions, and headers.
    *   Image optimization (alt text, compression).
    *   Review of internal linking strategy.
    *   Ensuring mobile-friendliness and fast loading times.
*   Integration of contact forms on all relevant service and contact pages.

### Out-of-Scope

*   Major architectural changes to the Next.js application beyond content structure.
*   Implementation of new backend systems (unless directly supporting new content features).
*   Major redesign of the overall website theme or primary layout (minor style adjustments are in-scope).
*   Off-page SEO activities (e.g., link building, social media marketing).
*   Complex interactive tools or calculators within content (unless simple forms).

## 4. Key Deliverables

*   Updated and new service pages.
*   Comprehensive Portfolio section with 3-5 detailed case studies.
*   Integrated client testimonials.
*   Optimized meta-data and content for target keywords.
*   Improved Lighthouse scores for performance and SEO.
*   Functional and compliant contact forms.

## 5. Success Criteria

*   Increased organic search traffic by X% within three months of deployment.
*   Improved average position for target keywords in search engine results.
*   Higher conversion rate on contact forms.
*   Positive feedback from stakeholders regarding the clarity and completeness of service and portfolio content.
*   All new/updated pages pass accessibility and SEO audits.
*   Adherence to all defined product and brand guidelines.
