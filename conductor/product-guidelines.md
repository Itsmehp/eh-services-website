# Product Guidelines: EH Services Portfolio & Showcase

## 1. Tone and Voice

All external-facing content, including website copy, marketing materials, and client communications, must maintain a **professional, authoritative, and informative** tone. The language should be clear, confident, and direct, emphasizing the expertise and reliability of EH Services.

## 2. Visual Identity and Aesthetics

The product's visual identity will be guided by the following principles:

*   **Design:** A clean, minimalist design is paramount. The focus should be on creating a seamless and intuitive user experience.
*   **Usability:** Every design choice must prioritize usability, ensuring that information is easy to find and understand.
*   **Typography:** We will use modern, legible typography that enhances readability and contributes to a professional aesthetic.
*   **Color Palette:** A specific, consistent color palette will be used across the entire product to ensure brand cohesion and recognition.

## 3. Data Handling and User Privacy

User trust is critical. Therefore, the product will adhere to the highest standards of data protection:

*   **Compliance:** We will strictly adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and all other relevant data protection laws.
*   **Transparency:** All data collection practices will be transparent. A clear, comprehensive, and easily accessible privacy policy will be provided for users.
*   **Consent:** User consent will be required for any data collection. Opt-in mechanisms will be clear and user-friendly.

## 4. Content Strategy for Services and Portfolio

To ensure maximum clarity and impact, the presentation of services and portfolio items will follow a structured approach:

*   **Service Offerings:**
    *   Each service will have its own dedicated page.
    *   Content will clearly articulate the benefits for the client.
    *   The process involved in delivering the service will be detailed.
    *   Each page will conclude with a clear call to action (e.g., "Request a Quote," "Schedule a Consultation").
*   **Portfolio Case Studies:**
    *   Case studies will be presented in a consistent format:
        1.  **Problem:** A clear description of the client's challenge.
        2.  **Solution:** A detailed explanation of the strategy and work delivered by EH Services.
        3.  **Results:** Tangible outcomes and metrics demonstrating the value provided.
        4.  **Client Testimonial:** A quote from the client to build social proof and credibility.
